Customer Service Guidelines and Policies:

  1. Customer Service Mission
    • Our mission is to provide exceptional customer service, ensuring a positive shopping experience for our customers while addressing their inquiries, concerns, and needs promptly and professionally.
  2. Communication Channels
    • Email Support: Response within 24 hours during business hours.
    • Phone Support: Call center available during business hours.
    • Social Media: Monitor and respond to inquiries on Instagram, Facebook and Twitter.
  3. Response Times
    • Email Support: Aim to respond within 24 hours.
    • Phone Support: Aim to answer calls promptly.
    • Social Media: Aim to respond within 24 hours.
  4. Product and Policy Knowledge
    • Customer service representatives should be well-versed in our products, services, and company policies. Regular training sessions will be conducted to keep the team up to date.
  5. Handling Inquiries
    • General Inquiries: Provide accurate and helpful information about products, services, promotions, and policies.
    • Order Inquiries: Assist customers with order status, tracking, and any issues related to the ordering process.
    • Returns and Refunds: Guide customers through the returns process and explain our return and refund policies.
    • Technical Issues: Troubleshoot and assist customers experiencing technical difficulties on our website.
  7. Professionalism and Courtesy
    • Address customers by name (if available).
    • Use respectful and professional language in all interactions.
    • Remain patient and empathetic, even in challenging situations.
  8. Escalation Process
    • For complex or unresolved issues, escalate to a senior customer service representative or supervisor.
    • Document the issue, steps taken, and any relevant information.
  9. Language and Multilingual Support
    • Use translation tools if necessary to ensure accurate communication.
  10. Personalization
    • Use customer data to personalize interactions.
    • Offer recommendations based on purchase history.
  11. Social Media Etiquette
    • Respond professionally and promptly.
    • If handling a complaint, offer to move the conversation to a private channel.
  12. Data Privacy and Security
    • Do not share sensitive customer information.
    • Follow our data privacy and security protocols at all times.
  13. Feedback and Improvement
    • Collect feedback from customers about their support experience.
    • Share feedback with the team for continuous improvement.
  14. Closing Interactions
    • Summarize the customer's concern and the resolution.
    • Thank the customer for their business.
